How does it work?

The impact tracker allows you to add commitments to actions that will have a positive impact on the world. After entering your commitments, our calculator will estimate how much of a positive impact your actions have had on the world!

Environmentally Conscious?

Keep track of how much your actions have had an impact on the environment such as greenhouse gas emissions and water usage!

Vegan or Vegetarian?

Using our impact calculator you can find out how many animals lives you've saved with your commitment!

Trying to reduce your water usage?

Find out how much water you've saved with your actions!
